Who: The course is aimed at:

  • All health care professionals - whether you are an allied health professional, nurse, pharmacist, health care scientist or doctor.
  • Clinical or non clinical researchers, at any career stage.
  • Those working in industry that use health data
  • PhD students
Please note priority to training will be given to health care professionals, researchers and industry partners first. You don't need to have any previous knowledge of the tools that will be presented at the workshop.
